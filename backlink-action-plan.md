# Purple Heart Limo — Backlink & Authority Action Plan

A plain-English, do-it-in-order plan to build trustworthy backlinks for
purpleheartlimo.com. Work top to bottom. The early items are free, fast, and
the highest impact for a local Texas limo company.

---

## PART 1 — Your NAP (use this EXACT text everywhere)

"NAP" = Name, Address, Phone. Google rewards businesses whose name, phone, and
location match **identically** across every site. Copy/paste this so it's always
the same — even one different format (e.g. "833.740.0700" vs "(833) 740-0700")
weakens the signal.

```
Business name:  Purple Heart Limo
Phone:          (833) 740-0700
Email:          info@purpleheartlimo.com
Website:        https://purpleheartlimo.com
City/Region:    Austin, Texas (also serving Dallas–Fort Worth & Houston)
Country:        United States
Tagline:        Texan-owned, veteran-founded luxury limo & black car service
```

> IMPORTANT: Many directories require a **full street address**. Your site only
> lists the city today. Decide on ONE official business address (even a registered
> business/mailing address) and use it identically everywhere. If you serve
> customers at their location (no walk-in office), most directories let you mark
> it as a "service-area business" and hide the street address — use that option.

## PART 3 — Local Relationship Links (the highest-quality links you can get)

These beat any directory because they're real businesses vouching for you.
A link from a venue or hotel near you is worth dozens of directory links.

- [ ] **Wedding & event venues** you already serve → ask to be listed as their
      "Preferred / Recommended Transportation Partner" with a link.
- [ ] **Hotels & resorts** (the ones in your blog guides — Kalahari, Universal Kids,
      DFW resorts) → offer to be their guest-transportation referral.
- [ ] **Event planners, photographers, DJs** → swap referrals + website mentions.
- [ ] **Corporate clients** (Austin tech corridor) → "trusted by" logo + link.
- [ ] **Sponsor a local event / charity / school prom** → sponsors get a link.
- [ ] **Local press / news** → pitch a timely angle (see World Cup 2026 below).

How to ask (copy/paste email):
> "Hi [Name], we're Purple Heart Limo — a Texan-owned, veteran-founded luxury car
> service that regularly brings guests to [Venue]. Would you add us to your
> preferred-vendors / transportation page? Happy to do the same for you. Here's our
> info: purpleheartlimo.com · (833) 740-0700."

## PART 4 — Your 5 Best "Link Magnet" Blog Posts

Don't build links TO blog posts directly. Instead, promote these 5 (they're your
deepest, most cite-worthy guides) so others link to them naturally. Each has a
clear outreach path — the people who would actually link.

1. **World Cup 2026 Dallas Limo Service Guide**
   `/blog/world-cup-2026-dallas-limo-service-guide/`
   WHY: AT&T Stadium (Arlington) is a 2026 World Cup host venue — massive, timely,
   newsworthy. WHO LINKS: local news, travel blogs, World Cup fan/travel guides.
   ACTION: Pitch local Dallas/Fort Worth media now, while interest is climbing.

2. **DFW Resorts Mega Guide 2026** (2,500+ words)
   `/blog/dfw-resorts-mega-guide-2026/`
   WHY: Big roundup/resource style — the format people cite. WHO LINKS: the resorts
   you mention, DFW travel/family blogs. ACTION: Email each resort featured: "We
   included you in our DFW resorts guide — feel free to share/link it."

3. **How Much Does Limo Service Cost in Austin 2026** (2,100+ words)
   `/blog/how-much-does-limo-service-cost-austin-2026/`
   WHY: Pricing/data guides are the #1 most-cited content type. WHO LINKS:
   journalists, budgeting/wedding blogs needing a cost source. ACTION: Submit to
   "expert source" services (e.g. journalist request platforms) as a pricing source.

4. **Best Wedding Venues in Dallas (DFW) Limo Service**
   `/blog/best-wedding-venues-in-dallas-dfw-limo-service/`
   WHY: Venue roundup = every venue named is a backlink target. WHO LINKS: the
   venues themselves. ACTION: Email each venue: "We featured you in our DFW wedding
   venue guide" → many will link back or add you as a vendor.

5. **Austin Wedding Limo — Hill Country Venue Guide** (2,000+ words)
   `/blog/austin-wedding-limo-hill-country-venue-guide/`
   WHY: Same venue-outreach angle for the Austin/Hill Country market. WHO LINKS:
   Dripping Springs / Fredericksburg / Wimberley wedding venues.

(Runner-ups worth promoting: Kalahari Round Rock Family Guide, Universal Kids
Frisco Guide, F1 COTA Austin Guide, SXSW Limo Guide — all event/destination posts
with natural outreach targets.)

## PART 5 — Internal Linking (DONE — already live in your code)

Every one of your 45 blog posts now has a "Keep Reading" section linking to 3
related posts (Austin posts → Austin posts, DFW → DFW, weddings → weddings, etc.).
This was the one link-building lever fully in your control, and it:

- keeps readers on the site longer,
- fixes the "pages with only one internal link" warning from SEMrush.

Your blog posts already link well to your money pages (Fleet, Booking, Rates,
Services, Service Areas), so that side is solid too.

## What to AVOID (these can get you penalized)
- Buying links / "1,000 backlinks for $20" services.
- Mass blog-comment or forum-signature spam.
- Irrelevant foreign directories.
- Listings with a phone/name that doesn't match your NAP exactly.
