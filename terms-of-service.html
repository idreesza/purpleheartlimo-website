Purple Heart Limo provides luxury ground transportation services in Austin, Dallas-Fort Worth, Houston, and surrounding Texas markets. By making a reservation with us, you agree to these terms and our pricing as quoted at time of booking.

Our commitment to you: The price quoted is the price you pay. All-inclusive means all-inclusive — gratuity, tolls, fuel surcharges, everything. We don't do surprise fees. Ever.

2. Reservations and Payment

Booking Confirmation

A reservation is confirmed when you receive written confirmation from Purple Heart Limo. Please review your confirmation carefully and notify us immediately of any errors. We confirm every booking within one hour during business hours.

Deposit and Payment

Payment terms vary by service type and are provided at time of booking. Major credit and debit cards are accepted. All payment information is processed securely through our booking platform partners.

Pricing

All quoted prices are all-inclusive and final unless you add services, additional stops, or extend hourly time beyond what was originally booked. No fuel surcharges, no tolls billed separately, no gratuity added at checkout. What we quote is what you pay.

3. Cancellation Policy

Standard Cancellations

  • 48+ hours before scheduled pickup: Full refund, no questions asked.
  • 24–48 hours before pickup: 50% refund or full credit toward future booking.
  • Less than 24 hours before pickup: No refund. The vehicle has been reserved and positioned for your ride.
  • No-shows: Full charge applies. If you're running late, please call us — we work with you when you communicate.

Event Cancellations

For weddings, proms, and other special events booked more than 30 days in advance, cancellation policies may differ. Full terms are provided at booking confirmation.

Cancellations by Purple Heart Limo

In the extremely rare event that we must cancel due to circumstances beyond our control, we will provide a full refund and make every reasonable effort to find alternative transportation for you.

4. Flight Tracking and Airport Transfers

For airport pickups, we track your flight automatically using the flight number provided. We provide up to 60 minutes of complimentary wait time from your actual landing time at no charge. This covers virtually every standard delay scenario. If your wait extends beyond 60 minutes, additional charges may apply at the hourly rate.

Please ensure your flight number is correctly entered at booking. Purple Heart Limo is not responsible for missed pickups resulting from incorrect flight information.

Damage to Vehicles

Passengers are responsible for any damage to the vehicle caused during their ride, including but not limited to stains, broken fixtures, or other damage requiring repair or cleaning. Cleaning fees start at $250 and vary by severity.

Alcohol Policy

Adults 21 and over may consume alcohol in our vehicles. We maintain a strict zero-tolerance policy for underage drinking — no exceptions, ever. Our chauffeurs have full authority to end a ride if this policy is violated. This is non-negotiable.

Smoking
