FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 48–72 hours in advance for standard requests, and 2–4 weeks for weddings and large events. Last-minute bookings are accommodated based on availability — just call us directly.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24+ hours before the scheduled pickup receive a full refund. Same-day cancellations may be subject to a partial charge. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel or modify.

Do you charge for flight delays?

Absolutely not. We track your flight and adjust automatically. There is no extra charge for flight delays — your chauffeur will be there whenever your flight arrives.

Are gratuity and fees included?

Our quotes include all applicable fees and taxes. Gratuity (15–20%) is not included but warmly appreciated by our chauffeurs. We always provide transparent, all-inclusive pricing.

Do you serve the Austin airport?

Yes! We serve Austin-Bergstrom International Airport (AUS) as well as private terminals and regional airports in the greater Austin area.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and Zelle. Corporate accounts can arrange monthly invoicing. A deposit may be required for large or long-duration bookings.
