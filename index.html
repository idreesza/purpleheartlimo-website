Airport Transfers

We serve all major Texas airports: Dallas/Fort Worth International (DFW), Dallas Love Field (DAL), Austin-Bergstrom International (AUS), George Bush Intercontinental (IAH), and William P. Hobby (HOU). We also handle private FBO and terminal pickups. City-to-city transfers between Austin, Dallas, and Houston are also available.

Yes! Our standard airport arrival service includes your chauffeur meeting you inside the terminal at baggage claim, holding a sign with your name. For international flights, they meet you outside the customs exit. This service is included in our airport transfer rates at no additional charge.

Booking & Cancellations

For airport transfers, 24-48 hours advance is recommended. For weddings, proms, and special events, book 4-8 weeks ahead as vehicles fill up fast. For World Cup 2026 matches, book immediately — we're filling up fast for the Argentina games and the Semifinal. We can book up to 12 months in advance.

Cancellations 24+ hours before your scheduled pickup are completely free. Within 24 hours may result in a charge up to the full reservation amount. A 20% non-refundable deposit applies to special event bookings (weddings, proms, stretch limos). Cancellations within 7 days of a special event may result in the full balance being charged.

For standard airport transfers and hourly bookings: no deposit required. For special events (weddings, proms, stretch limo bookings): a 20% non-refundable deposit is required to secure the vehicle. The remainder is charged the day before your event.

Due to high demand for stretch limos on Saturday evenings, we limit point-to-point limo reservations to be completed by 3pm and back in the general area, so we can prepare for the next reservation. For Saturday night events, hourly bookings are recommended.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 9, 2026

Purple Heart Limo ("Company," "we," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Reservation Data: Name, phone, email, pickup/drop-off addresses, date, time, passenger count, vehicle preference, special requests, and optional flight number.

Communications: Records of phone calls, SMS messages, emails, WhatsApp messages, and web form submissions.

Analytics: IP address, browser type, pages visited, referral source via Google Analytics and similar tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to process and confirm reservations, assign drivers, send confirmations via SMS and email, track flights for airport transfers, process payments, respond to inquiries, and improve our services.

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive transactional SMS including booking confirmations, driver details, and status updates. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. We do not sell phone numbers for marketing purposes.

We share your information only with service providers necessary to fulfill your reservation (reservation system, SMS provider, email platform, payment processor). We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. No internet transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You may request a copy, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting info@purpleheartlimo.com or calling +1 (833) 740-0700.

We use cookies for site functionality, preference storage, and analytics. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy
