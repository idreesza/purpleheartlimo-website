# Local SEO geo reality for purpleheartlimo.com

Purple Heart Limo's only verifiable physical presence is **Richardson, TX** (a Dallas-metro suburb). This determines where they can rank in Google's local "map pack" (the 3-pack with pins).

**Rule:**
- They CAN compete in the **Dallas / DFW map pack** — Richardson is metro Dallas, adjacent to the Park Cities, Plano, Frisco, Arlington corridor. A Google Business Profile (GBP) should be a **service-area business** based in Richardson covering DFW.
- They CANNOT realistically rank in the **Austin map pack** without a real Austin address/presence — map-pack results are tied to proximity to a verified location. Austin landing pages can still rank in **organic** (blue-link) results.

**Why:** Map-pack ranking requires a verified business location near the searcher; organic does not. Building Austin map-pack expectations on a Richardson-only business will disappoint.

**How to apply:** When advising on local SEO, separate "organic page" wins (works for both metros) from "map pack / GBP" wins (DFW only, until/unless an Austin location exists). As of June 2026 the client had **no GBP yet** — first GBP should be set up in Richardson as a service-area business.
