Legacy West

Legacy West Plano — DFW's Premier Mixed-Use Destination

Purple Heart Limo Team · Aug 2025 · DFW, TX

Adjacent to Toyota North America's stunning headquarters, Legacy West has attracted restaurants, retailers, and hotels that rival anything in the Metroplex. Legacy Hall alone — a 55,000 sq ft food hall with outdoor concert lawn — is worth the trip.

Legacy West Parking Reality

Legacy West's layout is designed for walking once you are there, but the parking situation during dinner hours and weekend afternoons is genuinely frustrating.

Key Highlights

  • Legacy Hall — 55,000 sq ft food hall with outdoor concert lawn, one of the best food halls in the US
  • Sixty Vines — wine bar with 60 wines on tap and serious food
  • The Rustic — Texas-casual dining with live music and a massive patio
  • Taco Deli — beloved Austin import, excellent tacos in a fast-casual format
  • Kona Grill — reliable upscale-casual dining with excellent happy hour
  • REI Co-Op — flagship-level outdoor gear store adjacent to the development
  • Porsche North Dallas nearby — Porsche Experience Center with driving tracks
  • Toyota North America HQ — stunning new campus that defines the area
  • Omni PGA Frisco Resort nearby — 500-room resort adjacent to PGA Frisco courses
  • Legacy Drive corridor — multiple luxury car dealerships and high-end retail

We navigate the Tollway access well and drop directly at Legacy Hall's main entrance. Many corporate clients in the Toyota and tech offices here use our sedan service for client pickups.
