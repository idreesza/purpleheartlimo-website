Fort Worth Stockyards: Everything You Need to Know Before You Visit

By Purple Heart Limo · Mar 2026 · Dallas-Fort Worth, TX

The Fort Worth Stockyards is one of the most authentic cultural experiences in Texas. Unlike manufactured tourist attractions, the Stockyards has operated continuously since 1866. Cowboys still work here. The cattle drive happens twice daily. Billy Bob's is the world's largest honky-tonk.

Getting to the Stockyards from Dallas

The Stockyards are 30 minutes west of downtown Dallas. If you're coming from DFW Airport, Dallas hotels, or anywhere eastern, a dedicated driver makes the trip seamless.

Key Highlights

  • Cattle Drive: 11:30am and 4:00pm daily, free to watch, absolutely essential
  • Billy Bob's Texas — 100,000 sq ft honky-tonk with indoor bull riding on weekends
  • Stockyards Hotel — staying the night here completes the experience
  • Mule Alley — revitalized exchange building with boutiques and restaurants
  • White Elephant Saloon — the legendary live music bar that defines the Stockyards
  • Texas Cowboy Hall of Fame — inside the historic horse and mule barns
  • Hyena's Comedy Club — live comedy on Exchange Avenue
  • Cowtown Cattle Pen Maze — surprisingly fun for adults and families
  • Vintage Steakhouse — dinner in the heart of the historic district
  • Fort Worth Herd — the only twice-daily cattle drive in the world

No parking to fight over (lots around the Stockyards fill fast on weekends), and you can enjoy Billy Bob's fully without someone staying sober for the drive back.
