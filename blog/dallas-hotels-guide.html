Best Luxury Hotels in Dallas — Where to Stay in Big D

Purple Heart Limo Team · Sep 2025 · DFW, TX

Dallas has Forbes-rated and AAA Five Diamond properties that compete with any city. Whether you are here for business or a special occasion, these are the hotels that define the Dallas experience.

Hotel Transportation That Matches the Property

Key Highlights

  • Rosewood Mansion on Turtle Creek — Forbes Five-Star, the historic pinnacle of Dallas luxury
  • The Crescent Court — Art Deco grandeur in Uptown, home to Nobu and The Spa
  • Hotel ZaZa — boutique personality with genuine style, popular for special occasions
  • The Joule — restored 1927 Gothic revival building in the Arts District
  • HALL Arts Hotel — luxury contemporary art-integrated hotel in the Arts District
  • The Adolphus — opened 1912, grand historic hotel in downtown Dallas
  • W Hotel Victory Park — near American Airlines Center, excellent for concerts and games
  • Thompson Dallas — newer luxury property in Uptown with excellent food and beverage
  • Virgin Hotels Dallas — stylish alternative hotel with great nightlife programming
  • The Highland Dallas — large luxury hotel in a landmark location
