What's Included in a Limo Rental in Dallas? The Complete Amenities Checklist

June 4, 2026 · 7 min read · Purple Heart Limo

Before you book, it's fair to ask exactly what you're paying for. With a Purple Heart Limo rental in Dallas, the core package always includes a professional licensed chauffeur, door-to-door service, flat-rate pricing, complimentary bottled water, and luggage assistance — with airport trips adding a 60-minute free wait and live flight tracking. This guide lays out the full checklist: what's always included, what you can add on request, what honestly isn't included, and what to expect from the moment you submit a booking. No fine print, no surprises — just a clear picture so you book with confidence on our website.

What's Always Included With Purple Heart Limo

Every rental, regardless of vehicle or occasion, comes with the essentials that define professional service:

  • Professional licensed chauffeur. A screened, uniformed, experienced driver — not a gig worker. Punctuality and discretion come standard.
  • Door-to-door service. We pick you up and drop you off exactly where you need to be, with the chauffeur handling the details.
  • Flat-rate pricing. The quote is the price. No surge, no demand multipliers, no surprise line items at the end.
  • 60-minute free wait on airport pickups. A delayed flight never means a missed ride or a penalty fee.
  • Live flight tracking. For airport runs, our flight tracker monitors your flight so your chauffeur adjusts automatically.
  • Luggage assistance. Your chauffeur loads and unloads your bags — you just step in.
  • Complimentary bottled water. Cold water is stocked in every vehicle.

For the full lineup of vehicles these amenities ride in, see our fleet page. The point of listing all of this is simple: with a reputable operator, none of it is an upcharge or a surprise. The chauffeur, the door service, the water, and the flat rate are the baseline you should expect from any quality Dallas limo company — not a premium tier you have to negotiate for. If a company treats the basics as add-ons, that tells you something about how they'll treat you on the day of your ride.

What's Available on Request

Beyond the standard package, you can tailor the ride to the occasion. These extras are confirmed in writing before you book so you always know the cost:

  • Additional stops. Add a pickup, a dinner stop, or a multi-venue route — just tell us in advance.
  • Decorations. Wedding, prom, birthday, or quinceañera touches inside the vehicle for the perfect photos.
  • Champagne and beverages. Where legally permitted and age-appropriate, we can help arrange refreshments for the cabin.
  • Child seats. Properly installed seats for families traveling with little ones.
  • Specific vehicle requests. Prefer the Mercedes S-Class over the Cadillac, or the Escalade over the Yukon? Ask and we'll match availability.

What's NOT Included (and Why)

Honesty builds trust, so here's the straight talk on what a limo rental doesn't cover:

  • Gratuity may be added separately rather than baked in — always check your written quote so you know which it is.
  • Alcohol itself isn't provided by us; we can accommodate refreshments where legal, but stocking liquor is the client's responsibility and never permitted for minors.
  • Parking, venue, or event fees charged by third parties (stadiums, hotels) aren't part of the limo rate.
  • Unapproved extra time or stops beyond your booked itinerary — though we'll always quote any additions transparently before they happen.

That transparency is the whole point. We'd rather tell you what isn't covered up front than surprise you at the end — that's the difference between a flat-rate company and an app that tacks on fees after the fact. How to Customize Your Ride for a Special Occasion

The easiest way to get exactly the experience you want is to tell us the occasion up front. A wedding, prom, anniversary, corporate event, or quinceañera each calls for different vehicle sizes, décor, routing, and timing — and we'll tailor all of it within a flat, written rate. Frequently Asked Questions

Is gratuity included in a Dallas limo rental?

Sometimes it's built into the quote and sometimes it's added separately, so always check your written quote. Purple Heart Limo states gratuity clearly up front, so you know exactly what's included before you book and there are no surprises at the end of the ride.

Does a limo rental include bottled water and chargers?

Yes. Every Purple Heart Limo vehicle is stocked with complimentary cold bottled water and phone chargers as part of the standard package, along with a professional chauffeur, door-to-door service, and luggage assistance. Airport trips also include live flight tracking and a 60-minute free wait.

Can I add decorations or extra stops to my Dallas limo rental?

Absolutely. Decorations, additional stops, child seats, and a specific vehicle request are all available on request and confirmed in writing before you book, so you always know the cost in advance. Just mention the occasion and what you'd like when you submit your request.
