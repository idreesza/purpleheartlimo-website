Sundance Square Fort Worth — The Ultimate Entertainment District Guide

Purple Heart Limo Team · Jun 2025 · DFW, TX

The result of 40 years of private investment by the Bass family, Sundance Square is clean, architecturally consistent, vibrant, and genuinely one of the best urban entertainment districts in Texas.

Dallas to Fort Worth Evening Run

Sundance Square is 30 minutes west of downtown Dallas. A DFW-to-Fort Worth evening is one of our most popular runs — start with dinner at Reata, catch a show at Bass Performance Hall.

Key Highlights

  • Sundance Square Plaza — outdoor space with concerts and events and a massive outdoor screen
  • Bass Performance Hall — acclaimed as America's Best Concert Hall by Texas Monthly
  • The Reata Restaurant — Texas cuisine at its most celebratory, open 25+ years
  • Del Frisco's Grille — reliable upscale-casual in a prime Sundance location
  • Flying Saucer Draught Emporium — 80+ beers on tap, best selection in Fort Worth
  • Billy Miner's Saloon — historic bar in a genuinely old building, great atmosphere
  • Scat Jazz Lounge — Fort Worth's best jazz club, intimate and excellent
  • Hotel Drover nearby in Mule Alley — the premier hotel experience near Fort Worth
  • The Modern Art Museum of Fort Worth nearby — Tadao Ando building, world-class collection
  • Fireside Pies — wood-fired pizza that pairs perfectly with a Sundance evening
