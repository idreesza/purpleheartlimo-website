Addison Guide

Addison Texas Restaurant Row — DFW's Dining Capital

Purple Heart Limo Team · Nov 2025 · Dallas-Fort Worth, TX

Addison hosts over 170 restaurants in a 4.4-square-mile area. Belt Line Road is the main artery, lined with everything from upscale steakhouses to Vietnamese pho to craft cocktail bars. This density is legitimately unique.

Addison's Perfect Limo Night

Because everything in Addison is so close together, a group can easily do drinks at one spot, dinner at another, and dessert somewhere else, all within half a mile.

What Makes This Worth Experiencing

  • Chamberlain's Fish Market Grill — Addison institution, James Beard-recognized
  • Coal Vines — pizza and wine in a cozy neighborhood setting
  • Jinbeh Japanese Restaurant — consistently excellent, local favorite for decades
  • Pappadeaux Seafood Kitchen — Louisiana-Cajun seafood at a very high level
  • Steve Fields Steak and Lobster Lounge — old-school Addison grandeur
  • Boi Na Braza Brazilian Steakhouse — churrascaria for groups
  • Gloria's Latin Cuisine — Salvadoran-Mexican fusion, extremely popular
  • The Londoner Pub — British-style pub with a genuinely excellent beer selection
  • Addison Improv — comedy club with consistently national touring acts
  • Vitruvian Park — beautiful post-dinner walk in the middle of Addison

We regularly run Addison dinner nights for groups, and it is one of our favorite itineraries because the proximity makes routing so easy.
