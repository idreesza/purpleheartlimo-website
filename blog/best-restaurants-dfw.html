Top 10 Restaurants in DFW Worth Arriving in a Limo

By Purple Heart Limo · Apr 2026 · Dallas-Fort Worth, TX

If you've ever pulled up to Al Biernat's in Dallas or Ellerbe Fine Foods in Fort Worth in anything less than a luxury vehicle, something already felt off. DFW is one of America's great dining cities, and your arrival should match the occasion.

Why Fine Dining Deserves Fine Transportation

Arriving at a world-class restaurant is itself part of the experience. Valet staff, maitre d's, the other guests — first impressions begin at the curb.

Key Highlights

  • Al Biernat's Oak Lawn — Dallas steakhouse royalty, power lunch institution
  • Ellerbe Fine Foods Fort Worth — farm-to-table with a Fort Worth soul
  • Knife Dallas — dry-aged beef at a genuinely extraordinary level
  • Bonnell's Fine Texas Cuisine — the best anniversary dinner in Fort Worth
  • Fearing's at the Ritz-Carlton — Dean Fearing's landmark Southwestern cuisine
  • Five Sixty by Wolfgang Puck — 560 feet above Dallas with a full city view
  • The Mansion at Rosewood Turtle Creek — Forbes Five-Star, always
  • Nobu Dallas — world-class Japanese in the Uptown Design District
  • Del Frisco's Double Eagle — where DFW business deals get closed
  • Saint Ann Restaurant & Bar — French-Texan fusion in the Arts District

A dedicated driver means the whole table can enjoy the wine list fully. No one has to volunteer to stay sober, and everyone gets home safely.
