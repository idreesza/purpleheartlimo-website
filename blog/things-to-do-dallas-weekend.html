20 Best Things to Do in Dallas This Weekend — 2026 Guide

By Purple Heart Limo · Apr 2026 · Dallas-Fort Worth, TX

Dallas moves fast. On any given weekend you might find yourself at the Mavericks game, then strolling the Perot Museum, then ending the night at a rooftop bar in Uptown. The city never really slows down.

Making the Most of Your Dallas Weekend

Dallas is a city where having a plan — and reliable transportation to execute it — turns a good weekend into a great one.

Key Highlights

  • Dallas Arboretum & Botanical Garden — stunning in every season
  • Sixth Floor Museum at Dealey Plaza — essential Dallas history
  • Deep Ellum Art Scene — galleries, murals, and live music
  • Bishop Arts District — boutique shopping and craft cocktails
  • Klyde Warren Park — the green heart of downtown Dallas
  • AT&T Stadium Tour Arlington — even on non-game days it's impressive
  • Dallas Museum of Art — free admission, one of the top 10 largest in the US
  • Trinity River Audubon Center — nature in the middle of the city
  • Perot Museum of Nature and Science — genuinely excellent for all ages
  • NorthPark Center — premier luxury shopping, free public art installation
