Dallas Restaurant Week — Your Complete DFW Dining Event Guide

Purple Heart Limo Team · Jul 2025 · DFW, TX

Dallas Restaurant Week happens in August and has grown into one of the most anticipated culinary events in Texas. Top restaurants offer $25 lunch and $35-45 dinner prix-fixe menus, making normally expensive spots accessible for one week.
