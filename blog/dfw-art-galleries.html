DFW Art Galleries — The Collector and Visitor Guide

Purple Heart Limo Team · Oct 2025 · Dallas-Fort Worth, TX

The Dallas-Fort Worth art market is one of the most active in the United States. Beyond the institutional museums, the gallery scene in Deep Ellum, the Design District, and Oak Cliff offers something more intimate and often more surprising.

An Art Gallery Tour Done Right

A Dallas Design District or Fort Worth Near Southside gallery tour is one of the most civilized ways to spend a Saturday. Most galleries are within walking distance of each other within their respective districts.

What Makes This Worth Experiencing

  • Conduit Gallery Dallas Design District — cutting-edge contemporary, excellent programming
  • Valley House Gallery Dallas — one of the oldest and most respected in the city
  • Barry Whistler Gallery Deep Ellum — influential gallery in a historic warehouse
  • Gerald Peters Gallery Dallas — major gallery handling historic and contemporary work
  • Ro2 Art Dallas — two locations, strong focus on emerging artists
  • Erin Cluley Gallery Dallas — design district gallery with excellent curatorial vision
  • Fort Works Art Fort Worth — exceptional contemporary gallery in Near Southside
  • William Campbell Contemporary Art Fort Worth — long-standing institution
  • Artspace111 Fort Worth — nonprofit gallery in a restored 1920s building
  • Galleri Urbane Dallas — modern and contemporary with international connections
