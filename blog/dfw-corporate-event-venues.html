Best Corporate Event Venues in DFW — Meetings, Conferences and Entertainment

Purple Heart Limo Team · Jul 2025 · DFW, TX

The Kay Bailey Hutchison Convention Center in downtown Dallas is one of the largest in the US. Beyond it, DFW has dozens of exceptional corporate event venues accommodating everything from board dinners to multi-day retreats.

Corporate Event Transportation at Scale

Corporate event transportation is a specialty at Purple Heart Limo. We coordinate multi-vehicle logistics for conferences, executive shuttles between hotels and event venues, VIP airport arrivals for keynote speakers.

Key Highlights

  • Kay Bailey Hutchison Convention Center Dallas — 2 million sq ft, one of the largest in the US
  • Gaylord Texan Resort Grapevine — massive resort with 465,000 sq ft of meeting space
  • Irving Convention Center at Las Colinas — 275,000 sq ft, major trade shows
  • Omni Dallas Hotel — attached to the convention center, 1,001 rooms and 110,000 sq ft of meeting space
  • AT&T Stadium — for truly exceptional corporate events, available for private rental
  • American Airlines Center Victory Park — stadium and arena both available for corporate events
  • HALL Arts Hotel and Conference Arts District — boutique luxury corporate events
  • The Joule Dallas — intimate luxury corporate dinners and small conferences
  • Perot Museum of Nature and Science — evening corporate events in a spectacular setting
  • Ritz-Carlton Dallas — 15,000 sq ft of meeting space in a Forbes Five-Star setting
