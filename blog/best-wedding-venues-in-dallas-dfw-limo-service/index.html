Best Wedding Venues in Dallas-Fort Worth — And the Limo Service That Gets You There in Style

May 2026 · 8 min read · Purple Heart Limo DFW

Dallas-Fort Worth hosts thousands of weddings every year, and the venue options range from historic landmarks to sleek modern spaces to sprawling garden estates. The DFW wedding market is sophisticated, competitive, and full of genuinely beautiful places to get married. The transportation question — how do you move your couple, wedding party, and guests between all these beautiful places on the most photographed day of your life — is something we help with every weekend.

We've served 500+ Texas weddings. Here's our curated guide to DFW's best wedding venues, matched with transportation insight for each one.

Dallas Wedding Venues

Historic Luxury · Downtown Dallas

The Adolphus Hotel

Dallas's grand dame of hotels. Built in 1912, the Adolphus brings Baroque grandeur to a downtown Dallas ballroom setting that photographs beautifully and carries genuine historical weight. The Century Room is the primary wedding venue space — ornate, spacious, spectacular. Airport transfer limos from DFW deliver out-of-town guests directly to the hotel's Commerce Street entrance in style that matches the venue.

Mansion Estate · Turtle Creek

Rosewood Mansion on Turtle Creek

The Mansion is one of the most celebrated hotels in the American Southwest — a 1925 Italian Renaissance mansion on the banks of Turtle Creek. For intimate weddings (150 guests or fewer) seeking an unimpeachable luxury setting, the Mansion is in a category by itself. Wedding party limos arriving at the Mansion's grand entrance create photographs that speak for themselves.

Modern Ballroom · Downtown Dallas

The Room on Main

A sleek, contemporary Dallas venue in the historic Preston Center building. The Room on Main works beautifully for modern couples who want an upscale venue without Victorian maximalism. The clean lines and beautiful natural light photography well in both daytime and evening setups. Central Dallas location makes it accessible from all corners of DFW.

Fort Worth Wedding Venues

Ballroom · Downtown Fort Worth

Ashton Gardens Fort Worth

Multiple ceremony and reception spaces in a grand estate setting. Ashton Gardens Fort Worth handles the logistics of large weddings particularly well — the capacity, the staff, and the on-site wedding coordination make it a reliable choice for couples who want beauty without having to coordinate every vendor independently. Limo service from nearby hotels rounds out the guest experience.

Historic Texas · Stockyards Area

The Stockyards Hotel

For couples drawn to Fort Worth's authentic Texas character, the Stockyards Hotel's historic spaces offer a one-of-a-kind wedding backdrop. The adjacent Stockyards National Historic District creates a guest experience unlike any other Texas venue — and arriving via stretch limo down Exchange Avenue is an entrance that absolutely no one forgets.

Wedding Transportation: What You Need to Know

  • Book early: Wedding vehicles — especially stretch limos, party buses, and Sprinters — book 2-4 months in advance for peak season weekends. In DFW, spring (April-June) and fall (September-November) are the busiest.
  • Coordinate with your planner: We work directly with wedding planners and day-of coordinators. The more information we have about the timeline, the more seamless the transportation will be.
  • Think about guests, not just the couple: Guest transportation from hotels to the venue and back is a genuine service that elevates the entire event. We coordinate multi-vehicle, multi-stop guest transportation routinely.
  • Backup vehicle: Ask any reputable limo company what their contingency plan is for a vehicle mechanical issue. We have backup vehicles on standby for every wedding commitment.
