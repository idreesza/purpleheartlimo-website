Lower Greenville Avenue Dallas — Neighborhood Bar and Restaurant Guide

Purple Heart Limo Team · Jun 2025 · DFW, TX

Stretching from Ross Avenue north to Mockingbird Lane, Lower Greenville is a mix of independently owned restaurants, craft beer bars, music venues, and Mexican food joints that together create one of the most authentic neighborhood experiences in the city.

Lower Greenville Is Dallas's Real Neighborhood

Lower Greenville is one of Dallas's most walkable bar corridors once you are there. Getting there and home requires a plan. Parking along Greenville fills by 8pm on weekends.

Key Highlights

  • Greenville Bar and Grill — the neighborhood anchor, been here forever, always will be
  • Terilli's — neighborhood Italian that Dallas has loved for 30 years
  • Jimmy's Food Store — Italian deli and wine shop institution on Bryan Street
  • The Old Monk — craft beer bar with the most extensive draft selection on Greenville
  • Maple Leaf Diner — the late-night poutine institution
  • Bryan Street Tavern — low-key neighborhood bar with a great beer garden
  • Cafe Brazil — 24-hour diner institution that Dallasites have been visiting since college
  • Granada Theater — the neighborhood's world-class live music venue
  • Taco Diner — reliable neighborhood Mexican with a great happy hour
  • Single Wide Deep Ellum nearby — the trailer park bar that should not work but completely does

We drop clients at the junction of Greenville and Belmont regularly and pick up from the same spot at closing time.
