Arts Calendar

DFW Arts Festivals and Cultural Events — The Annual Calendar

Purple Heart Limo Team · Jul 2025 · DFW, TX

DFW hosts some of the best outdoor art festivals, film festivals, and cultural celebrations in the South. The concentration of talent and investment in the arts here is growing every year.

Arts Festival Transportation

Arts festivals are some of our favorite events to support because the timeline is relaxed and the atmosphere is celebratory.

Key Highlights

  • Cottonwood Art Festival Richardson — one of the premier juried outdoor art festivals in the US, held twice annually
  • Artstillery Dallas Design District — large-scale arts festival
  • Main St. Fort Worth Arts Festival — 4-day outdoor festival, largest in the Southwest
  • Lone Star Film Festival Fort Worth Stockyards — annual film festival in a remarkable venue
  • Deep Ellum Arts Festival — 3-day arts and music festival in the most creative neighborhood
  • Texas Film Awards — annual celebration of Texas filmmakers
  • Oak Cliff Film Festival — independent film festival with genuine credibility
  • Dallas Art Fair — gallery-level contemporary art fair held annually
  • Allen Plano Symphony Pops — outdoor summer concert series
  • Perot Museum Gala — annual benefit and arts celebration

Whether arriving for the first day of the Cottonwood Art Festival or finishing an evening at Deep Ellum Arts Festival, a dedicated driver means you see more, stay longer, and do not worry about parking in a neighborhood not designed for 20,000 extra cars.
