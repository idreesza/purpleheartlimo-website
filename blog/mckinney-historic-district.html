McKinney Guide

McKinney Texas Historic District — Best Small Town in DFW

Purple Heart Limo Team · Dec 2025 · Dallas-Fort Worth, TX

McKinney's historic downtown square is lined with 1800s buildings housing antiques, restaurants, wine bars, and boutiques. On weekends it fills with everyone who needs a break from the intensity of Dallas proper.

Day Tripping to McKinney

McKinney is 35 miles north of downtown Dallas, a comfortable 45-minute drive that feels worlds away. We regularly take couples and families up for a weekend afternoon, especially for antique market days.

What Makes This Worth Experiencing

  • Historic Downtown Square — boutiques, antiques, restaurants in Victorian buildings
  • Hutchins BBQ — one of the top-rated BBQ spots in DFW, worth the drive alone
  • Rick's Chophouse — upscale steakhouse in a historic downtown building
  • McKinney Performing Arts Center — live theater in the old courthouse
  • Heard Natural Science Museum — surprisingly excellent, great for families
  • McKinney Warbird Museum — incredible WWII aircraft collection, volunteer-run
  • Collin County History Museum — traces the area to the 1840s
  • Adriatica Village — European-inspired neighborhood shopping center
  • Pantry Restaurant — beloved local brunch spot
  • Courthouse Square — the beautifully preserved centerpiece of downtown

Sedan service for two is the perfect way to spend a McKinney afternoon without coordinating a return drive.
