Christmas in Dallas-Fort Worth — Holiday Events, Lights and Celebrations 2025

Purple Heart Limo Team · Dec 2025 · DFW, TX

The combination of big-city resources and Texas personality means you get elaborate light installations at major venues, one of the top-rated holiday markets in the country, and a warmth that makes December in DFW genuinely special.

Christmas Light Tours We Love Running

Christmas limo rides are one of our favorite services. The holiday light displays at the Dallas Arboretum, Gaylord Texan, and Klyde Warren Park are genuinely better when you are not focused on where to park.

Key Highlights

  • Dallas Arboretum Garden of Lights — nightly light display through the botanical gardens, spectacular
  • NorthPark Center Holiday Display — free public art installation in one of the world's best shopping centers
  • Gaylord Texan ICE and Christmas at Gaylord Texan — massive event with 2 million pounds of sculpted ice
  • Southlake Town Square Tree Lighting — one of the most beloved suburban holiday traditions
  • Fort Worth Candlelight Tours — historic architecture tours of old Fort Worth
  • Klyde Warren Park Holiday Activation — downtown Dallas's outdoor park transforms completely
  • Sundance Square Tree Lighting Fort Worth — massive outdoor Christmas tree, a Fort Worth tradition
  • Bass Performance Hall Nutcracker — the Fort Worth Ballet's production is world-class
  • Dallas Symphony Holiday Concerts — multiple December dates at the Meyerson Symphony Center
  • Six Flags Holiday in the Park — theme park transforms for the holiday season

We run Christmas light tours throughout December. Pick a route, pick a vehicle, enjoy the season.
