Rooftop Bars

The Best Rooftop Bars in Dallas, TX for Skyline Views and Special Events

Purple Heart Limo Team · May 2025 · DFW, TX

Dallas's elevated drinking scene has developed considerably. From the high-altitude glamour of Five Sixty at Reunion Tower to neighborhood rooftop bars in Uptown and Lower Greenville, DFW's rooftop options are genuinely excellent.

Rooftop Bar Evenings and Limo Service

Rooftop bar evenings have a natural limo pairing. Dress code is higher, the experience is more elevated, and the cocktails are strong enough that the driving question becomes relevant.

Key Highlights

  • Five Sixty by Wolfgang Puck Reunion Tower — 560 feet, the highest cocktails in Dallas
  • Apotheke at Thompson Dallas Hotel — Uptown rooftop with a strong cocktail program
  • HG Sply Co Uptown — outdoor rooftop with health-conscious menu and great views
  • Bottled Blonde West Village — rooftop bar with Uptown views
  • Cosmo in the Cosmopolitan Deep Ellum — small rooftop in one of Deep Ellum's best buildings
  • Parliament Dallas — intimate rooftop bar with excellent small-plates menu
  • HIDE Dallas — newer rooftop with excellent natural wine program
  • Saint Ann Restaurant Arts District — rooftop overlooking the Arts District
  • Whiskey Cake multiple DFW locations — reliable upscale-casual with outdoor terrace
  • The Rustic Uptown — open-air festival seating that feels like a rooftop experience

We run rooftop bar evenings throughout DFW regularly, coordinating pickup times with the venue's last call to maximize your time with the view.

Best Rooftop Bars in Dallas for Special Events

Dallas rooftop bars are made for special events. The skyline views turn an ordinary night into something memorable, which is why so many groups choose a rooftop for birthdays, corporate happy hours, bachelorette parties, and date nights.

Most rooftop venues offer reservations for larger parties, and several pour an excellent list of cocktails and wines by the glass. Booking ahead matters on weekends, when the best tables with skyline views fill up fast.

  • Private and semi-private spaces for birthday and bachelorette celebrations
  • Corporate happy hours with skyline views as the backdrop
  • Date nights with cocktails and wines by the glass
  • Pre-dinner drinks before a downtown or Uptown reservation
