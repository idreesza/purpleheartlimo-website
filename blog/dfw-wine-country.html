Wine Tours

DFW Wine Country Day Trips — Grapevine to Fredericksburg

Purple Heart Limo Team · Dec 2025 · Dallas-Fort Worth, TX

Within 45 minutes of downtown Dallas, the Grapevine Wine Trail offers eight wineries within walking distance of each other. Stretch further and you find Denton and Waxahachie wine regions, both under an hour from DFW.

Why Wine Tours Need a Dedicated Driver

The whole point of a wine trail is that everyone gets to taste, not just the person staying sober. Our wine tour packages are designed so your whole group participates fully.

What Makes This Worth Experiencing

  • Grapevine Wine Trail — 8 wineries walkable, Historic Main Street backdrop
  • Delaney Vineyards Grapevine — one of the oldest and largest in North Texas
  • Inwood Estates Winery Dallas — urban winery in a restored warehouse
  • Lone Star Wine Cellars McKinney — 30 miles north, well-reviewed estate
  • Bingham Family Vineyards — DFW's most awarded producer
  • Fredericksburg Day Trip — 4 hours south, worth the full limo day trip
  • Messina Hof Bryan-College Station — on the way to Houston
  • Llano Estacado Winery — Texas's most awarded producer, longer trip
  • Wichita Falls Winery — 2 hours north, unique West Texas character
  • Hill Country Retreat — multiple wineries within walking distance in Fredericksburg

We have run the Grapevine trail dozens of times and can help plan the perfect winery order for the best experience.
