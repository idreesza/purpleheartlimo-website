Charity Gala and Fundraiser Transportation in DFW

Purple Heart Limo Team · Apr 2025 · DFW, TX

Dallas-Fort Worth's major charity galas — the Crystal Charity Ball, Children's Medical Center fundraisers, Dallas Foundation events — regularly attract $10,000-50,000 per table. Your arrival should match the occasion.

Black-Tie Events Require the Right Vehicle

Charity galas require a vehicle and driver that match the occasion. A Cadillac Escalade or Luxury Sedan, a uniformed chauffeur, and a punctual pickup that does not keep you rushing from a parking structure.

  • Crystal Charity Ball — one of the oldest and most prestigious charity events in Texas
  • Children's Medical Center Gala — annual black-tie benefit at major Dallas venues
  • Dallas Museum of Art Beaux Arts Ball — elegant arts fundraiser inside the museum itself
  • Nasher Prize Dinner — annual celebration of international sculpture
  • St. Jude Dream Home Gala DFW — annual large-scale fundraiser
  • American Heart Association Gala DFW — major annual fundraiser at Omni Dallas
  • Dallas Symphony Orchestra Gala — black-tie evening at the Meyerson Symphony Center
  • North Texas Food Bank events — increasingly ambitious fundraising events
  • Susan G. Komen Race DFW — morning event with coordinated group transportation
  • Salvation Army Gala Fort Worth — Doing the Most Good annual black-tie event
