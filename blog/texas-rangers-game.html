Texas Rangers at Globe Life Field — The Complete Fan Guide

Purple Heart Limo Team · Jan 2026 · Dallas-Fort Worth, TX

Globe Life Field's retractable roof means Texas summer heat is no longer an excuse to skip a game. The sight lines are excellent. But getting there and back can be seamless or a nightmare depending on your transportation plan.

Rangers Game Transportation Done Right

We have mastered the Arlington sports venue experience. We know which entrance approach avoids most pre-game traffic and where to hold during the game.

What Makes This Experience Worth It

  • Retractable roof — check if open for your game, completely changes the atmosphere
  • Live! by Loews Hotel connected to stadium via walkway — prime location
  • Arlington Entertainment District: Globe Life and AT&T Stadium within walking distance
  • Premium parking $35-60 depending on game day importance
  • Boomerjacks Grill and Bar — popular pre-game restaurant outside the park
  • Tailgating officially permitted in designated lots
  • Section 8 — Rangers supporters club section, loudest in the park
  • Post-game traffic: I-30 and SH-360 both back up 30-60 minutes
  • Texas Live! entertainment complex adjacent to both stadiums
  • Kids Zone on main concourse, great for younger fans
