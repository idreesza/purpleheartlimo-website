Grapevine Guide

Grapevine Texas — Wine, History and Weekend Fun

Purple Heart Limo Team · Jan 2026 · Dallas-Fort Worth, TX

Grapevine sits between Dallas and Fort Worth along Lake Grapevine and is genuinely one of North Texas's best-kept secrets. Historic Main Street is walkable and charming. The Grapevine Wine Trail includes eight wineries within walking distance of each other.

The Wine Trail Requires a Driver

Grapevine's wine trail is essentially impossible to enjoy properly if someone in your group has to stay sober. Our SUV wine tour packages are designed for exactly this.

What Makes This Experience Worth It

  • Historic Main Street — 1.5 mile walkable district with boutiques, galleries, and restaurants
  • Grapevine Wine Trail — 8 wineries walkable from each other on and near Main Street
  • Gaylord Texan Resort — massive indoor atrium resort on Lake Grapevine
  • Grapevine Vintage Railroad — steam train to Fort Worth Stockyards on select weekends
  • Harvest House on the Lake — lakeside event venue with incredible views
  • Grapevine Mills Mall — massive outlet and entertainment mall
  • Lake Grapevine — water activities, fishing, and boating
  • Nash Farm Battlefield — Civil War history in a surprising suburban location
  • Neel's Winery — one of the most visited on the trail
  • Main Street Cottages — charming overnight options in historic buildings

We know the trail well, know the best order to visit each winery, and make the whole afternoon feel exactly as relaxed as it should.
