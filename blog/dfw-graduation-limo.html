DFW Graduation Celebration — Luxury Limo for Your Big Day

Purple Heart Limo Team · Jun 2025 · DFW, TX

Graduation season in DFW spans May through August, covering high school ceremonies at AT&T Stadium and the American Airlines Center, and university ceremonies at UT Dallas, TCU, SMU, UTA, and dozens of others.

One Vehicle Eliminates All the Logistics

We see it every graduation season: families trying to coordinate multiple vehicles across a ceremony venue and a dinner reservation. Someone gets lost, someone is late, the photos are rushed.

Key Highlights

  • UT Dallas Graduation — Eisemann Center Richardson, December and May ceremonies
  • TCU Commencement — Amon Carter Stadium Fort Worth, May and August
  • SMU Graduation — Gerald J. Ford Stadium Highland Park, typically May
  • UTA Commencement — College Park Center Arlington, May and December
  • Texas Woman's University Denton — May ceremonies
  • High School Graduation at AT&T Stadium — multiple DFW schools use the Cowboys stadium
  • American Airlines Center High School Graduations — June and July dates
  • Celebration Dinner: Legacy West, Uptown Dallas, Sundance Square Fort Worth all excellent
  • Post-Graduation Party Venues — Klyde Warren Park, rooftop event spaces across DFW
  • The Gift: many families give a limo evening as the graduation present — this is a great idea

Our graduation packages solve this completely. Everyone in one vehicle, on time, with a driver who knows the venue entrances.
