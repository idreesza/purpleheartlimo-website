Brewery & Wine

DFW Brewery and Vineyard Tours — The Craft Beer and Wine Guide

Purple Heart Limo Team · Feb 2025 · DFW, TX

DFW now has over 60 craft breweries, multiple urban wineries, and the Grapevine Wine Trail, all accessible within the Metroplex. Building a tour day around these is one of the most enjoyable ways to spend a weekend.

Brewery Tours and Dedicated Drivers

A brewery or winery tour is incompatible with designated-driver logistics. The whole premise requires your whole group gets to taste everything.

Key Highlights

  • Deep Ellum Brewing Co. — one of the oldest and most respected craft breweries in Dallas
  • Peticolas Brewing — award-winning Belgian-influenced brewery in Dallas Design District
  • Lakewood Brewing Garland — one of DFW's most decorated craft breweries
  • Community Beer Dallas — excellent local brand with a beautiful taproom
  • Revolver Brewing Granbury — Texas-focused craft brewery with strong distribution
  • Grapevine Wine Trail — 8 wineries walkable from Historic Main Street
  • Inwood Estates Winery Dallas — urban winery in a restored warehouse
  • Delaney Vineyards Grapevine — one of the oldest Texas wine producers
  • True Vine Brewing Tyler — worth the 2-hour day trip if you are serious about Texas beer
  • Lone Star Wine Cellars McKinney — 30 minutes north of Dallas, well-reviewed estate

Our brewery tour packages solve this beautifully. We build the route and everyone in the vehicle gets to be a participant.
