Bishop Arts

Bishop Arts District Dallas — The Bohemian Heart of Oak Cliff

Purple Heart Limo Team · Sep 2025 · DFW, TX

The Bishop Arts District in Oak Cliff is independently owned, locally loved, and genuinely unlike anywhere else in Dallas. Lucia, Eno's Pizza, The Wild Detectives bookshop-bar, Emporium Pies — this is the real Dallas.

Bishop Arts Parking Situation

Bishop Arts is a narrow two-block district with almost no parking and it is always busy on weekends. Even on a Tuesday evening, finding street parking within a block of Lucia requires more luck than most visitors want to count on.

Key Highlights

  • Lucia — James Beard-nominated, one of the genuinely best restaurants in Texas
  • Eno's Pizza Tavern — wood-fired pizza in a neighborhood institution
  • Boulevardier — classic French bistro that would not feel out of place in Lyon
  • Lockhart Smokehouse — Texas BBQ in a landmark brick building
  • Emporium Pies — the pies alone are worth the trip to Oak Cliff
  • The Wild Detectives — bookshop and bar designed in a dream
  • Hattie B's Hot Chicken — Nashville hot chicken that draws the whole city to Oak Cliff
  • Serious Pie — Seattle import, excellent pizza in a renovated building
  • Bishop Arts Theater Center — intimate live theater in the heart of the district
  • Oak Cliff Brewing Company — local craft beer from one of Dallas's most interesting neighborhoods

Having a driver drop you at Bishop and Davis, and picking you up from the same spot three hours later, is the most civilized way to experience Bishop Arts.
