Best Route: Round Rock to AUS

Three viable routes from Round Rock to Austin-Bergstrom; our chauffeurs choose based on time-of-day traffic:

  1. TX-130 toll road (recommended for rush hour): ~40 min, bypasses I-35 entirely. Tolls included in our flat rate.
  2. I-35 South + TX-71 East: ~45 min off-peak, can extend to 70+ min during morning rush hour gridlock.
  3. MoPac Express + TX-71 East: ~50 min, useful as a backup if I-35 is severely impacted.

How much does a limo from Round Rock to Austin-Bergstrom Airport cost?

Purple Heart Limo's flat-rate Round Rock to AUS transfers start at $165 for an executive sedan, $195 for an executive SUV (Escalade), $225 for a luxury Navigator, and $215 for a 14-passenger Mercedes Sprinter. All rates include chauffeur, fuel, tolls, flight tracking, and bottled water.

How long does it take to drive from Round Rock to AUS airport?

The Round Rock to Austin-Bergstrom drive is approximately 30 miles. Via the TX-130 toll road (our recommended route during rush hour), the drive takes 40-45 minutes. Via I-35 South + TX-71 East, expect 45 minutes off-peak or up to 70+ minutes during morning rush hour gridlock.

What's the best route from Round Rock to Austin-Bergstrom?

During rush hour (6:30-9 AM and 4-7 PM), TX-130 toll road is fastest — bypasses I-35 entirely, typically 40 minutes flat. Off-peak, I-35 South to TX-71 East is shortest at ~45 minutes. Our chauffeurs choose the optimal route in real time based on traffic conditions.
