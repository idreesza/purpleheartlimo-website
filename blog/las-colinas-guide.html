Las Colinas

Las Colinas Texas — Business District and Entertainment Guide

Purple Heart Limo Team · Nov 2025 · Dallas-Fort Worth, TX

Las Colinas sits between Dallas and DFW Airport and is home to Fortune 500 headquarters, the Toyota Music Factory, a stunning canal walk modeled after Venice, and some of the best hotel infrastructure in DFW.

Las Colinas and DFW Airport

Las Colinas is the most common destination for business travelers flying into DFW who have meetings before checking into a hotel. Our DFW-to-Las-Colinas transfers are among our most frequent runs.

What Makes This Worth Experiencing

  • Mandalay Canal Walk — gondola rides and waterfront dining in the middle of Irving
  • The Mustangs of Las Colinas — famous large-scale bronze sculpture, must-see public art
  • Toyota Music Factory — world-class concert venue and entertainment complex
  • Irving Convention Center — regular host of major trade shows
  • Four Seasons Resort and Club — world-class golf and spa 30 minutes from DFW Airport
  • Flix Brewhouse Las Colinas — dine-in cinema with craft beer brewed on-site
  • Omni Las Colinas Hotel — major corporate hotel adjacent to the convention center
  • Water Street Las Colinas — new development with boutique dining
  • Las Colinas Urban Center — office and retail district with excellent restaurants
  • Kimberly-Clark and Celanese corporate HQs — part of DFW's Fortune 500 concentration
