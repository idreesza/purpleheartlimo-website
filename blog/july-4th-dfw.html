Fourth of July in DFW — Best Fireworks and Celebrations 2026

Purple Heart Limo Team · Aug 2025 · DFW, TX

With a Metroplex population approaching 8 million, the scale of DFW July 4th celebrations matches the population. The challenge is always transportation home when 200,000 people are trying to leave at the same time.

Kaboom Town Requires Pre-Planned Transportation

Kaboom Town in Addison draws 100,000 people to a 4-square-mile city. Getting out without a pre-planned arrangement is a genuine ordeal.

Key Highlights

  • Kaboom Town Addison — consistently ranked among the top July 4th shows in the US, draws 100,000+
  • Dallas Cowboys 4th of July at AT&T Stadium — visible for miles
  • Panther Island Pavilion Fort Worth — festival on the Trinity River with fireworks
  • Southlake Town Square — suburban family-friendly celebration
  • Frisco Freedom Fest at The Star — major patriotic celebration
  • Plano Balloon and Music Festival — large-scale festival at Oak Point Park
  • Mesquite Championship Rodeo — July 4th rodeo is a Texas classic
  • Greenville Avenue festivities — neighborhood-scale celebration with local energy
  • Garland July 4th Spectacular — one of the larger suburban shows in DFW
  • Fort Worth Cultural District area — typically hosts public Independence Day events

We offer July 4th packages every year and strongly recommend booking your fireworks night transportation at least two weeks in advance.
