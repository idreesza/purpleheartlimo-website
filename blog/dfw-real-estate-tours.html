DFW Neighborhood Tours for Home Buyers — Moving to Dallas Guide

Purple Heart Limo Team · Aug 2025 · DFW, TX

Dallas-Fort Worth consistently ranks among the fastest-growing metros in the United States. Understanding the different neighborhoods and suburbs requires actual time on the ground, and a 9,000-square-mile metro does not orient itself quickly.

Real Estate Tours in a Cadillac Escalade

Real estate professionals in DFW use Purple Heart Limo for client neighborhood tours regularly. When a client flies in from San Francisco or New York to explore options, a day in an Escalade visiting Highland Park, Uptown, Frisco, and Legacy West gives a real feel for the Metroplex.

Key Highlights

  • Highland Park — most exclusive enclave, old-money Dallas with top-rated schools
  • University Park — adjacent to SMU, slightly more approachable than Highland Park
  • Uptown Dallas — urban lifestyle, walkable, skews younger professional
  • Preston Hollow — large estates, privacy, celebrity and executive concentration
  • Lakewood — east Dallas neighborhood with 1920s-40s home stock, great character
  • Bishop Arts and Oak Cliff — creative, eclectic, most rapidly appreciating area in Dallas
  • Frisco — master-planned suburb, excellent schools, explosive growth
  • Allen and McKinney — northern DFW suburbs with great value and community feel
  • Southlake and Westlake — premier luxury suburbs in Tarrant County
  • Plano Legacy West — high-end suburban lifestyle with major corporate employment nearby
