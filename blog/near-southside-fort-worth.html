Near Southside Fort Worth — The Creative Neighborhood Guide

Purple Heart Limo Team · May 2025 · DFW, TX

Magnolia Avenue is the main artery of Fort Worth's Near Southside, lined with restaurants ranging from Vietnamese pho to French-inspired wine bars to some of the best Mexican food in Tarrant County.

Dallas to Near Southside Fort Worth

Near Southside is 3 miles south of downtown Fort Worth, about 10 minutes from Sundance Square. We regularly build Fort Worth itineraries that include both Magnolia Avenue for dinner and the Stockyards for late night.

Key Highlights

  • Magnolia Avenue — the main commercial street, dense with independent restaurants and bars
  • Brewed Coffee and Tea — the neighborhood's social center, breakfast and brunch done right
  • Fixture Kitchen and Social Lounge — one of Fort Worth's most sophisticated restaurants
  • Winslow's Wine Cafe — neighborhood wine bar with excellent by-the-glass program
  • Spiral Diner and Bakery — beloved vegetarian restaurant, been here forever
  • Little Red Wasp — live music venue and bar with real neighborhood character
  • Benito's — Fort Worth Mexican institution on Magnolia, family-run for decades
  • Paris Coffee Shop — no-frills diner that has been here since 1926
  • Fort Works Art — excellent contemporary gallery with strong programming
  • The Near Southside culture — a neighborhood that still has genuine character

It is one of the best complete evenings DFW has to offer.
