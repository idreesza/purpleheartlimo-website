Best Brunch in DFW — 2026 Dallas Fort Worth Brunch Guide

Purple Heart Limo Team · Nov 2025 · Dallas-Fort Worth, TX

Weekend brunch lines in Uptown Dallas form before 10am and the best spots fill completely by 11. DFW's brunch scene is deep enough to occupy your entire weekend and then some.

Brunch Transportation That Makes Sense

Brunch is one of our favorite services because the timing works perfectly. Getting a group to a restaurant that opens at 10 without someone having to drive and stay sober is genuinely better with a chauffeur.

What Makes This Worth Experiencing

  • Bread Winners Cafe Uptown Dallas — the quintessential Dallas brunch institution, expect a wait
  • CBD Provisions at The Joule — farm-to-table brunch in a luxury hotel setting
  • Sadelle's Highland Park — New York import, bagel towers and caviar service
  • Hattie B's Hot Chicken Dallas — Nashville hot chicken that draws the whole city
  • Cane Rosso Deep Ellum — Neapolitan pizza brunch, stranger than it sounds and completely perfect
  • Ellen's Southern Kitchen Dallas — massive biscuits, incredible chicken and waffles
  • Whiskey Cake multiple DFW locations — reliable, excellent, great for groups
  • Brewed Fort Worth Near Southside — serious brunch menu in a great neighborhood
  • Fixture Fort Worth Sundance Square — sophisticated brunch in a beautiful space
  • The Usual Dallas — neighborhood vibe with unexpectedly excellent food

We run brunch outings regularly for birthdays, baby showers, and post-event mornings throughout DFW.
