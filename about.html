Our Story

About Purple Heart Limo

Texan-owned. Professionally operated. Committed to excellence on every ride across Austin, DFW & Houston.

Who We Are

Built on Service,
Driven by Excellence

Purple Heart Limo was founded by a United States military veteran with a simple mission: to bring the same discipline, reliability, and commitment to service that defines military life to the luxury transportation industry across Texas.

The name "Purple Heart" is a tribute to the valor and sacrifice of America's service members. Every ride we provide is delivered with that same sense of honor — because you deserve nothing less than the best.

From a single vehicle to a growing fleet serving Austin, Dallas-Fort Worth, and Houston, we've built our reputation one exceptional ride at a time. Our clients return because they trust us — with their time, their safety, and their most important moments.
