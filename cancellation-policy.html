Policy

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Clear, fair, and straightforward. We believe you should fully understand our cancellation terms before you book — not after something changes.

Last updated: May 2026

Standard Cancellation Policy

Purple Heart Limo operates on a flat-rate, pre-booked model. Because we assign specific vehicles and drivers to your booking and decline other bookings for that slot, cancellations affect our operations directly. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both parties.

Cancellation TimingRefund
More than 72 hours before scheduled pickupFull refund
48–72 hours before scheduled pickup75% refund
24–48 hours before scheduled pickup50% refund
Less than 24 hours before scheduled pickupNo refund
No-show (driver arrives, client not present)No refund

How to cancel: Call us at (833) 740-0700 or email info@purpleheartlimo.com with your booking confirmation number. Cancellation time is measured from when you contact us, not when we respond.

Special Event Policy

The following event types have modified cancellation terms due to the demand nature of the booking windows:

Weddings

Wedding transportation bookings require a 25% non-refundable deposit at time of booking to secure the date. The remaining balance is due 7 days before the event.

  • Cancellation more than 30 days before: Deposit forfeited, remaining balance refunded
  • Cancellation 14–30 days before: 50% of total booking refunded
  • Cancellation less than 14 days before: No refund

Prom Night

Prom bookings follow standard cancellation terms, with a 20% non-refundable deposit required at booking. Given that prom season is our highest-demand window, we strongly recommend booking early and planning carefully before reserving.

Major Texas Events (SXSW, ACL, F1)

Event-week bookings require full payment at time of booking and are non-refundable within 7 days of the event date. Cancellations more than 7 days before the event receive a 75% refund.

Rescheduling

We understand plans change. Rescheduling is treated differently from cancellation:

  • Rescheduling requests made more than 48 hours in advance: Free of charge, subject to vehicle availability
  • Rescheduling within 24–48 hours: $50 rescheduling fee
  • Rescheduling within 24 hours: Treated as a cancellation and a new booking

Our commitment: If we ever need to cancel your booking — due to a mechanical issue or an emergency — you receive a full refund immediately, no questions asked. We maintain backup vehicle options for exactly this reason, so a cancellation from our side is extremely rare.

Weather & Force Majeure

In the event of severe weather, natural disaster, or other force majeure conditions that make safe transportation impossible, bookings may be rescheduled without penalty or refunded at 100%. Safety is always the priority. Call us immediately if you have concerns about weather on your event date.

Contact Us About Your Booking

If you have questions about a specific booking or need to discuss options, we prefer to talk directly rather than process forms. Call (833) 740-0700 or email info@purpleheartlimo.com — a real person answers.
